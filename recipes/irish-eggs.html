Irish EGGS

A great way to use up leftover baked potatoes, this hearty breakfast scramble also has a touch of Irish green thanks to the addition of bell peppers.

Ingredients

  1. 2 tablespoons butter
  2. 6 potatoes, peeled and sliced
  3. 1 onion, minced
  4. 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  5. 6 eggs, beaten

Directions

  • In a large skillet, melt butter over medium high heat. Add potatoes, onion and green pepper; saute until potatoes are browned. Stir in eggs and cook until eggs are set. Serve warm.
